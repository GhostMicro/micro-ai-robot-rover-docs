    üîç Troubleshooting

    "Failed to connect"

    Solutions: 1. Check TX/RX swap (TX‚ÜíRX, RX‚ÜíTX) 2. Verify IO0 is shorted to GND 3. Press RESET while IO0 grounded 4. Check 3.3V power (not 5V on VCC pin!)

    Cause: Not enough power
    Solution: Use external 5V 1A supply

    "Invalid head of packet"

    Cause: Wrong baud rate
    Solution: Use 115200 (default)
